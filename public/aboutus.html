About Us

At AgroSight, we are committed to providing a technological solution that helps predict prices of essential agri-horticultural commodities. Our approach is driven by Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, ensuring that the predictions we offer are as accurate and timely as possible. We have collaborated with the Ministry of Consumer Affairs of India to solve a key challenge they face: predicting commodity prices to maintain price stability.

The Ministry of Consumer Affairs is tasked with maintaining stable prices for everyday commodities like grains, vegetables, and pulses. They manage a buffer stock of these essential goods to prevent price spikes. When prices rise, buffer stock is released into the market, increasing supply and stabilizing the market. However, their current algorithms, which use factors such as seasonality, crop estimates, and historical trends, are not always precise in predicting future price fluctuations. This is where AgroSight comes in with a better, more advanced solution.

Our AI-ML model analyzes a variety of factors such as market trends, historical data, and production estimates, providing accurate price predictions for the upcoming months. With this model, we aim to support the government in making informed decisions about when and how much buffer stock to release, preventing drastic price hikes before they occur.

In addition to our predictive models, we are also developing a comprehensive web portal with two user levels: citizens and government authorities. Citizens will be able to access past and present price data for major commodities, while government officials will have access to predictive dashboards that suggest whether to release buffer stock, and if so, what percentage of the stock to release. This level of insight enables the Ministry to maintain price stability more efficiently and effectively than ever before.

Through this solution, AgroSight aims to empower the Ministry of Consumer Affairs to make data-driven decisions in maintaining fair prices for essential goods, ensuring food security for the nation.
